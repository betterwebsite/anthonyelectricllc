About Anthony Electric

We’re a locally owned business based in Sand Springs, Oklahoma, built on honest work and reliable service. We believe that every home and business deserves safe, efficient, and properly installed electrical systems. With years of hands-on experience, we handle everything from simple fixes to full-scale upgrades with professionalism and precision.

Safety First

And Why It Matters

Electrical safety isn’t optional—it’s essential to protect your home, your family, and your property. That’s why following proper codes, standards, and regulations is at the core of everything we do. Cutting corners with wiring or ignoring updates to the National Electrical Code can lead to serious hazards, but with Anthony Electric LLC, your system is built to meet and exceed all safety requirements.

We stay up to date on the latest electrical regulations to ensure every installation and repair is safe, legal, and built to last. Whether it's a new panel, upgraded wiring, or a simple outlet fix, we double-check every detail for code compliance. Safety isn't just a checklist—it's our standard. The First Time.
